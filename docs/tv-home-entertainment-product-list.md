## How to use this list

This is deliberately broader than what should launch first — cross-reference against the priority comparisons in `tv-home-entertainment-content-plan.md` (Samsung vs LG, OLED vs QLED, TCL vs Hisense, Roku vs Fire TV Stick, etc.) to pick the specific models for the first batch of pages. General pattern for picking a comparison pair: match tier-to-tier (flagship vs flagship, budget vs budget) since that's what a real buyer is actually deciding between — e.g. `Samsung S95H vs LG G6` (flagship OLED-class) reads a real buyer intent, while `Samsung S95H vs TCL Q7D Pro` (flagship vs budget) does not.
